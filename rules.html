Rules:






1 - Be polite to other people. Offenses will not be tolerated.
2 - Do not steal or destroy other people's buildings
3 - Even with this rule 2, protect your buildings
4 - Do not leave holes on the surface. And try to replant the cut trees.
5 - Towers of width of 1 node will be destroyed and the author will suffer a punishment. So avoid it.
6 - Houses "floating" on water will also not be tolerated, except
in the shape of ships or something in the real world that floats on water.
7 - Also no farms floating on the water. Make an embankment to the bottom of the water body or an island.
8 - If you intend to do something floating in the air, zeppelins or blimps are allowed. No
castles or houses in the air. Airspace is used by flying machines, therefore it is controlled.
9 - You can start with a small construction, but one that is aesthetically beautiful. Ugly buildings
will receive a sign notifying them of the need for improvement. If nothing is done, it will be demolished.
10 - Upon being noticed the use of hack, the user will be banned.


Follow the rules and have fun.
